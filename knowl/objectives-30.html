Objectives 6.2.

  • To understand how the Laplace transform acts on derivatives.
  • To understand how the Laplace transform can be used to solve initial value problems such as
    \begin{align*} a y''(t) + b y'(t) + c y(t) \amp = f(t)\\ y(0) \amp = y_0\\ y'(0) \amp = y'_0 \end{align*}
    even when \(f(t)\) is discontinuous.
