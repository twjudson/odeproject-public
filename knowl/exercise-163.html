Exercise 1.

Consider a cooperating species model
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = x(1-x) + \beta xy\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = y(1-y) + \beta xy, \end{align*}
where the species interact weakly, say \(\beta = 1/2\text{.}\)
  1. Find the \(x\) and \(y\)-nullclines for this system.
  2. Find all equilibrium points for this system.
  3. Sketch the phase plane for this system.
  4. If the initial populations are given by \(x(0) = 2.4\) and \(y(0) = 0.3\text{,}\) what happens to the two populations as \(t \to \infty\text{?}\)
