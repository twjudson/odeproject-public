Consider the system
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = y\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = - \sin x. \end{align*}
  1. Verify that the function
    \begin{equation*} V(x, y) = \frac{y^2}{2} - \cos x + 1 \end{equation*}
    is a Lyapunov function for the system, and calculate \(\dot{V}(x,y)\text{.}\)
  2. Sketch the level sets of \(V\text{.}\)
  3. Sketch the phase portrait of the system.
