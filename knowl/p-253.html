It is easy to generate a phase line diagram for equation (1.3.4) from the graph of \(f(y) = y^2(y + 2)(y - 2)\) (Figure 1.3.12). If the graph is above the \(y\)-axis, then \(y\) is increasing. If the graph is below the \(y\)-axis, then \(y\) is decreasing. Therefore, the phase line is easy to sketch (Figure 1.3.13).
