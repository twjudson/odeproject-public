Exercise Group 5.3.6.10–13. Gradient Systems.

Which of the systems in Exercise Group 5.3.6.10–13 are gradient systems. If the system is a gradient system, find a gradient function.
10.
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = 12x^2 - 6 xy^2\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = -6x^2y + 2y \end{align*}
11.
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = 10xy - y \cot x\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = 5x^2 - \tan x \end{align*}
12.
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = 2 \cos x \cos y\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = - 2 \sin x \sin y. \end{align*}
13.
\begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} & = 3x + 4y\\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = -4x + 3y \end{align*}
