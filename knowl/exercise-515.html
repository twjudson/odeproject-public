Exercise 1.

Consider the system
\begin{align*} x' & = y\\ y' & = -x - \frac{y}{4} + x^2. \end{align*}
  1. Verify that the function
    \begin{equation*} L(x, y) = \frac{x^2}{2} + \frac{y^2}{2} - \frac{x^3}{3} \end{equation*}
    is a Lyapunov function for the system.
  2. Sketch the level sets of \(L\text{.}\)
  3. What can you conclude about the phase portrait of the system from (1) and (2)?
