Activity 6.1.1. Finding Laplace Transforms.

Find the Laplace transform for each of the functions below. Recall that \(\cosh t = (e^t + e^{-t})/2\) and \(\sinh t = (e^t - e^{-t})/2\)
(a)
\(f(t) = t^6\)
(b)
\(f(t) = t^7 e^{-t}\)
(c)
\(f(t) = 3\sin^2 \left(\sqrt{2}t\right)\)
(d)
\(f(t) = e^{3t}\sin 2t\)
(e)
\(f(t) = 2 t^2 \cosh t\)
