A linear planar system
\begin{align*} x' & = ax + by\\ y' & = cx + dy \end{align*}
has an equilibrium solution at \((x_0, y_0)\) if
\begin{align*} a x_0 + b y_0 & = 0,\\ c x_0 + d y_0 & = 0. \end{align*}
