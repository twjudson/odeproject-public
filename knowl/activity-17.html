Activity 2.2.1. Plotting Direction Fields.

Plot direction fields for each of the following systems of differential equations in the \(xy\)-plane.
(a)
\begin{align*} x' & = -3x\\ y' & = 4y \end{align*}
(b)
\begin{align*} x' & = 2x\\ y' & = y/2 \end{align*}
(c)
\begin{align*} x' & = x/2\\ y' & = 2y \end{align*}
(d)
\begin{align*} x' & = x + y\\ y' & = x - y \end{align*}
